# Annual Coffee Sales Revenue Analysis

## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of **annual coffee sales revenue** using transactional sales data. The objective is to uncover revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and payment preferences through a fully interactive **Microsoft Excel dashboard**.

The analysis is designed to support **data-driven business decisions** by transforming raw sales transactions into actionable insights that can guide pricing strategies, inventory planning, and customer engagement initiatives.

## Problem Statement
Coffee businesses generate large volumes of transactional data daily, yet without proper analysis, critical insights remain hidden. Decision-makers often struggle to answer key questions such as:
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- When do sales peak during the year?
- How do customers prefer to pay?
- Which customers contribute most to revenue?

This project addresses these challenges by analyzing coffee sales data to **optimize revenue performance**, **understand customer behavior**, and **improve strategic planning**.

## Objectives
The primary objectives of this analysis include:
- Understanding overall sales revenue and performance trends
- Identifying best-selling and high-revenue products
- Analyzing customer purchasing patterns
- Evaluating payment method distribution and preferences
- Comparing sales performance across months, days, and years
- Highlighting top customers and product contribution to revenue

## Dataset Description
- **Data Type:** Transactional sales data  
- **Time Coverage:** Annual  
- **Key Fields Include:**
  - Transaction Date
  - Product Name / Category
  - Unit Price
  - Quantity Sold
  - Sales Revenue
  - Payment Method
  - Customer ID
- **Data Preparation:**
  - Removal of duplicates and invalid records
  - Standardization of date formats
  - Creation of calculated fields for KPIs and revenue metrics
  - Validation of sales totals and pricing consistency

- **Data Analysis Techniques**
  - Descriptive analysis

## Dashboard Overview
The Excel dashboard provides an interactive view of coffee sales performance, featuring:

- **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
  - Total Sales Revenue
  - Average Sales Value
  - Total Customer Footfall

- **Sales Analysis Visuals:**
  - Sales Revenue by Year
  - Monthly Sales Trends
  - Sales by Day of the Week

- **Product Performance:**
  - Revenue contribution by product
  - Quantity sold by product
  - Price vs revenue comparison

- **Customer & Payment Insights:**
  - Payment method distribution
  - Top revenue-generating customers
  - Customer purchase frequency

- **Interactive Controls:**
  - Slicers to filter by time period, product, and payment method

## Key Insights
- Certain coffee products consistently outperform others, contributing the highest share of total revenue.
- Sales peak during specific months, indicating seasonal purchasing behavior.
- Weekdays show stronger sales performance compared to weekends.
- Digital and card-based payments dominate customer payment preferences.
- A small segment of customers contributes a disproportionately large share of total revenue.
- Higher-priced products do not always generate the highest revenue, highlighting the importance of sales volume.

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products to maximize revenue impact.
- Introduce targeted promotions during low-performing months to stabilize sales.
- Optimize inventory levels based on peak sales periods and best-selling items.
- Encourage preferred payment methods through incentives or loyalty programs.
- Develop customer retention strategies for high-value customers.

## Business Value
This analysis delivers value by:
- Enabling data-driven sales and pricing decisions
- Improving understanding of customer behavior and preferences
- Supporting revenue optimization and demand planning
- Providing stakeholders with a clear, visual summary
